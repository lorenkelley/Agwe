Where You Want To Stay

Agwe hotels was created in 2017 for the modern jet setter. With locations in Trinidad, Cuba, and Costa Rica, the tropics come alive when you stay at Agwe! Agwe was created to make your travel experince more personal and less stressful. Our boutique hotels are immersed within the culture and local communities, so that you get an authentic experince. At we not only provided a place for you to stay (home away from home ), we create curated travel experinces for every guest. Once you booking is proceesed we send a optional (short!) questionaire about what you want to get out of your vacation, purely rexlation , adventure, or a mix of both. We then create complinetry sample itineries for you.
